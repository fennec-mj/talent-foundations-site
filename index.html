FAQ

Not sure what we need yet?

That’s normal. Most clients start with a few advisory sessions and we figure it out together.

Do you only work on recruiting?

No. I support hiring, talent strategy, people systems, and early people operations.

Do you replace an internal recruiter or People leader?

Yes, on an interim or fractional basis for one client at a time. I step in to run hiring, improve the process, align interviewers, and hire your internal recruiting team so hiring runs well long after I step back.

Can you work alongside our recruiters or agencies?

Yes. Often I help founders get more value and better outcomes from existing partners.
