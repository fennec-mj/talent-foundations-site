About Megan

I’ve spent my career building high-performing technical teams inside fast-scaling companies.

I hired Tesla Autopilot’s deep learning team during the shift from classical computer vision to deep learning, scaled autonomy software teams at Aurora Innovation through rapid growth and IPO, and led the zero-to-100 talent build at Atomic Semi as Fractional Head of Talent.

Working inside these teams showed me a consistent pattern: early hiring decisions and people systems compound. When the foundations are strong, teams move faster and execute better. When they’re not, friction shows up everywhere.

Today, I help startups design custom hiring and people foundations from first principles — so they can execute their vision and build companies as thoughtfully as the products they create.

FAQ

Not sure what we need yet?

That’s normal. Most clients start with a few advisory sessions and we figure out priorities and scope together from there.

How long are engagements?

It varies. Some founders bring me in for focused hiring or infrastructure sprints. Others work with me longer term in an advisory or fractional capacity. We right-size the engagement to what the company actually needs.

When should we bring you in?

Usually earlier than you think. The highest leverage point is when you’re hiring your first critical roles or scaling beyond founder-led recruiting. Early decisions compound — for better or worse.

Do you only work on recruiting?

No. I support hiring strategy, talent systems, interview design, recruiting operations, and early people infrastructure — not just role execution.

Do you replace an internal recruiter or People leader?

Sometimes on a fractional or interim basis. I can step in to stabilize hiring, improve the process, and help you hire the right long-term owner. The goal is to build a system that runs well without depending on me.

What makes your approach different from a recruiting agency?

Agencies focus on filling roles. I focus on helping you design the hiring and people system behind the roles — how decisions are made, how talent is evaluated, and how the process scales well. Candidate flow is only one piece.

Do you recruit directly?

Yes, depending on the engagement. When I do, it’s paired with process and system design so you’re not starting from scratch on every hire.

Can you work alongside our recruiters or agencies?

Yes. I often help founders get better signal, alignment, and outcomes from existing recruiters or agency partners.
